Background

I am Anthony a Nigerian Frontend developer and ux designer based in Nigeria, i have been practicing designs for a little over 9months, however i recently dived into programming. My skillset in designs spans from Ui designs to Ux designs, i'm currently a freelancer. Much of my designs have been targetted at designing and developing easy to use and self explanatory user experince.

My design journey started with me enrolling for the google Ux design course, and after almost 6months i completed it and was ready to explore the world with my newly acquired skill. After months of making beautiful designs i decided to improve my skillset by not just being able to design beautiful user experiences but also being able to bring them to life through programming, it was then i started taking courses on Udemy and after months i enrolled in a physical bootcamp where i acquired more knowledge on Html, Css and Javascript and many more. Ever since then i have been working on improving my programming skills and also my design skills.

I am passionate about designs and also bringing designs into full fuctionality with my programming skill, i believe learning never ends and i'm constantly on a mission to make life easier for users and also to improve my skillset.
